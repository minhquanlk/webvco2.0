WHO WE ARE LOOKING FOR?

  • Currently live in Ho Chi Minh City.

Professional Skills Requirement

  • Communication: Skills to convey information clearly and effectively, both in speaking and writing, and including listening comprehension skills.
  • Problem Solving and Decision Making: Ability to quickly identify problems and make wise decisions to solve them.
  • Analytical Thinking: Understanding of consumer trends and behavior.
  • Persuasion and Negotiation: Ability to persuade individual customers quickly and effectively.
  • Product Knowledge: Understanding of the product or service well enough to explain and advise accurately and effectively.
  • Handling Situations: Ability to handle negative feedback or objections positively and build trust.
  • Active Listening: The ability to listen carefully and understand the customer's needs and problems.

Technical Skills Requirement

  • Social Media Savvy: Proficiency in using social media platforms for marketing and sales purposes.
  • Reporting and Spreadsheet Skills: Proficiency in using tools like Microsoft Excel và Google Apps for sales reporting and analysis.
  • Basic Marketing Skills: Understanding of basic marketing principles and digital marketing tactics such as email marketing, SEO, and content marketing.
  • Make decisions based on data.

Responsibilities and Duties

  • Responsible for selling Education Event to students is responsible for developing and implementing sales strategies to effectively market and sell the workshops.

Key Activities

  • Search for and source VCO's potential student customer base by building and maintaining relationships within the student community.
  • Consult, discuss, and answer customer questions about products/services.
  • Multi-Channel Communications: Develop communication campaigns through multiple channels such as social networks, email, online forums, and word-of-mouth events to reach a broad audience.
  • Track, analyze, and report on sales performance, as well as understand trends and opportunities through data analysis.
  • Collect feedback from customers after workshops to improve products and customer experiences in the future.
  • Provide after-sales support to ensure customers have a positive experience and are willing to participate in subsequent Education Event .
  • Support event and workshop.

Benefits
